Frequently Asked Questions

How does ShortMyLink.in work?

You shorten a link using our platform, and we display a short ad before redirecting users to the final URL. You earn money from those ads.

How do I earn money from my links?

Every time someone clicks your shortened link and views the ad page, you earn a portion of the ad revenue. Earnings are tracked in real time.

How much commission does ShortMyLink.in take?

We take a small 15% commission on your ad revenue. You keep 85% of what you earn.

When and how will I get paid?

Payouts are made once your earnings reach the minimum threshold. We support payments via UPI, PayPal, and bank transfer (coming soon).
